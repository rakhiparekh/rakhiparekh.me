Adventures in Body Scanning

Buying Denim is one of the toughest shopping tasks for a woman. So when I heard about Bodymetrics, the new body scanning capsule that makes denim recommendations, I was curious. It sounded too good to be true and so I headed to Bloomingdales in Palo Alto, to test it out.

I documented my journey and spoke with the representative from Bodymetrics. The story was published in digitalstyledigest.com, a leading destination exploring the space where fashion meets technology.
