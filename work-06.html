Over the past decade I worked as a design Entrepreneur wihtin the Fashion, Retail and Advertsing industries. Through these experiences, I developed a keen instinct for grasping emerging trends in the popluar culture.
My entreprenuerial pursuits taught me to seek a balance between business strategy and creative thinking.

As a co-founder of a Fashion styling agency, I styled advertign campaigns, editorials and films for my clients. For these projects I collaborated with Creative Directors, Brand Managers, Editors and Filmmakers. The deadlines were demanding and requirements all consuming. My passion for saying yes to a challenge kept me going.
