Responsive Web-design

www.mylosolutions.com

A responsive web-design for Mylo Solutions, Inc.
an Enterprise Software Company

I worked closely with the marketing and business development team at Mylo to redesign the website and the logo. The goal was to design a solution that communicates the product in a who, what, where and why narrative.

I designed and built the responsive website using HTML5, CSS3, jQuery & Javascript. This project was executed over a period of 5 weeks.

The strategy for the redesign was to use visuals and content blocks to seperate bits of information that can be easily consumed. The experience strategy is to convey the message in a transparent and friendly manner.
